Platform Capabilities
Intelligent Automation
🤖
AI Auto-Scheduling
Automatically generates optimal daily schedules using demand prediction, historical ridership data, and real-time traffic — no manual intervention needed.
🗺️
Dynamic Route Management
Reroutes buses in real-time around accidents, road closures, and traffic jams. Adds or removes routes based on live demand signals.
📡
Real-Time Fleet Tracking
GPS-based tracking of every bus with 30-second updates. Dispatchers see live positions, ETAs, and deviations on the central dashboard.
🔧
Predictive Maintenance
IoT sensors monitor bus health. The system auto-schedules maintenance before breakdowns occur, minimizing downtime and delays.
📊
Ridership Analytics
Stop-level ridership data, crowd forecasting, and revenue analytics help planners make evidence-based route and frequency decisions.
🔔
Automated Alerts
Instant notifications to depots, drivers, and passengers when delays, breakdowns, or schedule changes occur. Multi-channel delivery via app, SMS, and display boards.
